Sënple/Sample

Below is a translation of The lord's prayer in Fränð to help understand the syntax

Our Father, who art in heaven, hallowed be thy name; thy kingdom come; thy will be done on earth as it is in heaven. Give us this day our daily bread; and forgive us our trespasses as we forgive those who trespass against us; and lead us not into temptation, but deliver us from evil. Amen.

Nötre päre, kï ä ö çiœ, krï çoæ çon nön; le roæjöme vië; ï tú vá être fini çür tèrre köm çä ö çiœ. Döń nü çe ðür nötre piin ańuel; Ï pardoń nü pür nötre iintrüðjon köm nü pardonöń çœ kï iintrüðjë köntre nü; ï mänë nü pa dën la tëntatïjoń, mä nü lïvrën dü mauvä. Ámë.

Fráse’ de báse’/Basic Sentences

Salü = Hello

Komë çá vá? = How’s it going?

Märçï bökó = Thanks so much.

Ðapréçï vrämë… =  I really appreciate…

Sküse = Excuse me.

Désölé(e) = I am sorry.

K’ë pëse tú? = What do you think?

Komë çá çone? =  How does that sound?

Çá çone bjen = That sounds great.

Non, çä rjë = Oh, never mind.
